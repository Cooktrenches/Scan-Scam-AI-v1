[‚ùì] Frequently Asked Questions

How do I interpret the Risk Score vs AI Score?

Risk Score (0-100): Traditional analysis based on holder concentration, liquidity, and red flags. Lower = safer.
AI Score (0-100): Machine learning prediction trained on 1000+ historical tokens. Higher = more likely legitimate.

Both scores complement each other - consider both when making decisions!

What does "Bundle: Yes" mean?

"Bundle: Yes" indicates that multiple wallets purchased the token in a coordinated pattern at launch. This could mean:

  • Insider trading: Dev and friends buying before public announcement
  • Bot activity: Automated systems splitting buys across wallets
  • Bundled launch: Token sold in pre-arranged bundles

Bundle detection is a red flag but not always a scam - investigate further!

What's a Sybil Attack?

A Sybil Attack is when a scammer creates many fresh wallets to hold the token, making it appear well-distributed while actually controlling most of the supply. Our scanner detects this by:

  • Checking wallet ages in top 20 holders
  • Flagging when 14+ out of 20 are brand new
  • Combining with bundle detection analysis

How accurate is the AI prediction?

Our Random Forest and Gradient Boosting models achieve ~87% accuracy based on historical data. However, scammers constantly evolve their tactics, and very new tokens may have insufficient data. The "Analysis Confidence" score tells you how reliable the prediction is for that specific token.

What should I do if a token shows low scores?

If both Risk Score and AI Score are low:

  • DO NOT invest based on FOMO
  • Check the red flags list carefully
  • Research the team and project independently
  • Look for bundle detection and sybil warnings
  • Ask yourself: "Would I be okay losing this investment?"

Remember: Low scores don't guarantee a rug, but they indicate high risk!

Why isn't my scan working?

Common issues and solutions:

  • Invalid address: Make sure you're using the correct Solana token address
  • Very new token: Tokens under 1 minute old may not have enough data yet
  • Network issues: Solana RPC can be slow during high traffic
  • Bot timeout: Scans take 10-30 seconds - please be patient!
